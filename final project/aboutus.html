Open and in business since 1987, we have been the source for farm fresh fruits and vegetables year round! We have partnered with local farmers to ensure prime pickings. We started with a local restaurant and found the need too feed to great for one spot.

2007, we started catering to the whole area with our food trucks! No longer did anyone have to travel a great distance and risk spoiling the freshness. We come to you! You can find our trucks located all over SoCal. Just keep your eyes peeled! 😉
